About FoodLens

FoodLens is an innovative AI-powered application designed to assess the quality and freshness of perishable food items. By combining advanced image recognition technology with an intuitive user interface, FoodLens helps consumers make safer, more informed decisions about the food they eat. Our goal is to reduce food waste, promote food safety awareness, and empower individuals to better understand the lifespan of their produce.

Our Mission

We believe technology should make everyday choices smarter, safer, and more sustainable. FoodLens represents our shared commitment to innovation, education, and real-world impact. Our mission is to harness the power of artificial intelligence to bridge the gap between technology and everyday life, creating a tool that promotes awareness, accessibility, and sustainability in food consumption.

Through this project, we aim to inspire change in how people think about their food by empowering them to minimize waste, make informed choices, and gain confidence in the quality of what they consume. By combining data science, user-centered design, and creative collaboration, we strive to make FoodLens not just an app, but a step toward a smarter, more sustainable future.
